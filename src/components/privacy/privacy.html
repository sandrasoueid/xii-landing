Peterbook's Privacy Principles

Peterbook was built to promise users a secure and safe social platform to connect with friends and family. It is important that you are aware of how we handle your data in Peterbook.

Privacy Control

  • Our groups feature allows users to decide who they share their posts with

Complete data control

  • When you delete a post, photo, or your profile, Peterbook permanently deletes your data from our systems.

Control how you share

  • Choose who gets see your posts
  • Manage how your profile is viewed by others.
